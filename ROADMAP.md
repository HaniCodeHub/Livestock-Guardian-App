# Project Roadmap

## Mission Statement

To revolutionize livestock management and ownership verification through biometric technology, enabling farmers to protect their animals, verify ownership, and build a more secure and transparent agricultural ecosystem.

## Vision (2025-2026)

- Become the leading livestock identification platform in developing nations
- Achieve 100,000+ registered animals globally
- Expand to 50+ countries
- Integrate with livestock markets and agricultural organizations
- Support multiple livestock species and farmer cooperative networks

## Dependencies & Risks

### Critical Dependencies
- Supabase platform stability
- AI model performance
- Network connectivity in target regions
- User adoption in emerging markets

### Identified Risks
- **Model Accuracy**: Risk of low identification accuracy
  - Mitigation: Continuous model training, data collection
- **User Adoption**: Difficulty convincing farmers to adopt new technology
  - Mitigation: Free tier, partnerships with agricultural organizations
- **Scalability**: Performance issues with large user base
  - Mitigation: Load testing, infrastructure scaling plan
- **Data Privacy**: Regulatory compliance in different countries
  - Mitigation: Legal review, GDPR compliance
