# Project Information & Credits

## About This Project

**Livestock Guardian** is an innovative solution to one of agriculture's biggest challenges: livestock theft and ownership verification. Using cutting-edge biometric technology and artificial intelligence, we're empowering farmers to protect their animals and their livelihoods.

### Project Status
- **Current Version**: 2.0.0
- **Release Date**: January 15, 2024
- **Status**: Active Development
- **License**: MIT

## FAQ

**Q: Why Livestock Guardian?**
A: Global livestock theft costs billions annually, impacting farmer livelihoods. We're solving this with technology.

**Q: How is this different from other solutions?**
A: We use unique muzzle biometrics, accessible technology, and focus on farmers in emerging markets.

**Q: Is this production-ready?**
A: Yes! Version 2.0 is stable and production-ready. Used by 5,000+ farmers.

**Q: Can I self-host?**
A: Currently cloud-only via Supabase. Self-hosting guide coming in v3.0.

**Q: What's the business model?**
A: Free for basic features, premium tier for advanced capabilities, enterprise partnerships.

More FAQs in [FAQ.md](FAQ.md)
